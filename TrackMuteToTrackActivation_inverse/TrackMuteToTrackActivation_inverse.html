TRACK MUTE TO TRACK ACTIVATION - INVERSE

Bitwig Studio controller script, intended to enable muting the note output of note-only tracks.

However, this is achieved in a VERY QUICK AND DIRTY manner, by linking the track mute state for FOR ALL TRACKS (i.e. also audio-only tracks) in Bitwig Studio to the activation state of tracks (/ channels).

The (maximum) number of tracks (excluding master track and effect tracks) being observed can be configured by the user.
It is recommended to set this to a relatively small number of tracks, and reserve the same amount of tracks in (all) your projects for notes-only tracks.

WARNING: may lead to 'hanging' notes sent between tracks (similar to 'hanging' MIDI notes), and also 'hanging' notes within the native instrument devices (which is a bit different; the *envelopes* of the notes also seem to 'hang').

NB: also see the "TrackMuteForNoteOutput" sibling script for a slightly more sophisticated approach, that does *not* affect audio-only tracks.
WARNING: Do not use these two scripts simultaneously!
